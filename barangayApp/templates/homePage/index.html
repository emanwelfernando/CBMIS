    About

    CBMIS or Comprehensive Barangay Information Management System is a web-based application aimed at efficiently managing household profiling in Biliran Province. It serves as a centralized platform for accurate household profiling, collecting demographic data on various categories such as persons with disabilities, single mothers, senior citizens, and unemployed individuals. The system features refined search queries based on specific criteria, providing relevant results.
